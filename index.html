📜 Rules & terms
Free to enter — strictly no buy-ins, no side bets. One entry per person; your real Instagram handle is your identity and a 4-digit PIN protects it. Predict every match. Predictions lock at kickoff. Scoring: see the full points table under "How do points work?" on the Matches screen. In short: group matches give +3 for the right result and +2 for the exact score; knockout games give +4 to +10 for picking who goes through, plus an optional exact-score bonus and an exact-score streak bonus on top; the champion pick is worth +25. Ties open for picks once both teams are confirmed. Champion pick: +25 points — locks on Thursday 18 June, 7:00 PM Doha. Results are official FIFA full-time scores. Prizes: 1st — a Maldives trip for one with 3-night accommodation included; 2nd — 60,000 QNB Life Rewards points; 3rd — 40,000 QNB Life Rewards points. Prize ties resolve by most predictions made, then most exact scores, then most correct results, then earliest sign-up. Pool admins play for bragging rights only and are not prize-eligible. Winners are verified by DM from their Instagram account and an audit of their entry before any award. Tampering with other players' picks or entering predictions after lock by any means leads to removal. The organizer may correct errors and has final say. Your handle, display name, department and country are visible to other players; all data is deleted after the final.
